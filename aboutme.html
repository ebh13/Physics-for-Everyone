Physics for Everyone

About Me

Hi there — I'm the creator of this project. My name is Emilia Boy, and I believe everyone should be able to explore the universe, regardless of background or resources.

I’m a student, a dreamer, and someone deeply passionate about science, especially physics. I started this platform after realizing how difficult it was for many people to get clear, hands-on explanations of even the most basic principles. What started as personal notes and sketches grew into this site.

My goal is simple: to break down the invisible walls that keep people away from physics. Whether it's due to financial constraints, learning differences, or just bad experiences in school, this is a place where science is rebuilt from the ground up to be exciting, understandable, and empowering.

My Vision

I want this to be more than just a website. I want it to be a movement, one where curiosity is enough to begin. By combining interactive tools, stories, and open-source access, this project is built to invite people in — not push them away. And I’m just getting started.

If this has helped you in any way, or if you'd like to contribute, reach out. Together, we can bring more people into the fold of science and discovery, and have fun doing it.
